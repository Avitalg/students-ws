Get Student By Id

you can find a student by his Id, by entering the following URL: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getStudent/id

Example: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getStudent/1 and the result will be 

{
student_id: 1,
name: "tom",
area: "center",
degree: "bsc",
grade: 81,
year: 1
}

get All Excellence Students

you can find all excellence students, by entering the following URL: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getAllExcellenceStudents

Get Excellence Students by Year

you can find a student by their year in school, by entering the following URL: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getExcellenceByYear/year

year could be a number between 1 and 4, for example: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getExcellenceByYear/1 and the result will be 

[
{
student_id: 3,
name: "bar",
area: "north",
degree: "bsc",
grade: 92,
year: 1
}
]

Get Student By Degree and Year

You can get student by degree and year by entering the URL bellow: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getStudent/:degree/:year

Student with range of grades

To find students that their grades between a range, you can enter the URL bellow: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getGrade/grade1/grade2

For example, to find students that their grade between 50 and 70, you should enter the URL: https://wstudents.herokuapp.com/getGrade/50/70

The result will be:

[
{
